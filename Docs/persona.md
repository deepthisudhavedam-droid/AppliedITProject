## Product Persona Document
## Persona 1: Maya Chen - The Busy Urban Professional
- **Age:** 28
- **Role:** Marketing Manager
- **Context:** Tech-savvy but struggles with "decision fatigue" when looking at her wardrobe. She uses the system to maximize her existing clothes and save time during her busy mornings.
- **What she loves:** The "Smart Weather Sync" feature that adjusts recommendations based on the forecast.
- **What she finds confusing:** The "Color Harmony" logic when it suggests bold, experimental combinations that push her out of her comfort zone.


Scenario 1: Maya's Rainy Workday
Actor: Maya Chen.
Goal: Find a professional outfit suitable for a rainy commute and an important meeting.
Setting: Her bedroom, 7:30 AM, raining outside.
Action: Maya opens the app; it detects the local weather and filters her wardrobe for "Rainproof" and "Business Casual" tags.
Outcome: She selects a suggested trench coat and waterproof boot combo in seconds, arriving at work on time and dry.
