Vision Statment
||

Personal Wardrobe Scanning — Upload photos of your clothes; AI catalogs items by color, style, fit.

Smart Outfit Mixing — AI suggests complete looks using only your existing wardrobe pieces.

Occasion Matching — Input event type (work, party); get tailored combos instantly.

Virtual Try-On — Preview outfits on your body for perfect styling.

Sustainability Focus — Zero new buys — maximize what you own, reduce waste. 

||

# Project Personas

## Persona 1: Maya Chen - The Busy Urban Professional
- **Age:** 28
- **Role:** Marketing Manager
- **Context:** Tech-savvy but struggles with "decision fatigue" when looking at her wardrobe. She uses the system to maximize her existing clothes and save time during her busy mornings.
- **What she loves:** The "Smart Weather Sync" feature that adjusts recommendations based on the forecast.
- **What she finds confusing:** The "Color Harmony" logic when it suggests bold, experimental combinations that push her out of her comfort zone.

## Persona 2: Alex Rivera - The Eco-Conscious Minimalist
- **Age:** 24
- **Role:** Graduate Student / Environmental Activist
- **Context:** Wants to reduce consumption by repurposing existing garments. Values sustainability and longevity.
- **What they love:** The "Refresh" feature that suggests new ways to wear old items.
- **What they find confusing:** The "Cost-per-wear" calculation when it doesn't account for second-hand purchase prices easily.

## Persona 3: Jordan Lee - The Aspiring Style Influencer
- **Age:** 21
- **Role:** Fashion Student
- **Context:** Loves experimenting with styles for social media content. Needs inspiration for niche aesthetics.
- **What they love:** "Experimental Mode" for bold, high-contrast outfit suggestions.
- **What they find confusing:** Navigating the tag system for very specific sub-cultures (e.g., "Cyber-Core").
